How to Join The Gaming Crib Server: Step-by-Step Guide

Welcome to The Gaming Crib! Joining our Minecraft server is simple and fun. Follow this guide to get started.

Step 1: Choose Your Minecraft Edition

We support both Java Edition and Bedrock Edition. Confirm which edition you have to connect properly.

Step 2: Get the Server Address

Our server address is: play.thegamingcrib.net. You will use this to add our server to your Minecraft multiplayer list.

Step 3: Add the Server

  • Open Minecraft and go to the Multiplayer menu.
  • Click “Add Server”.
  • Enter the server name (e.g., The Gaming Crib).
  • Paste the server address play.thegamingcrib.net in the Server Address field.
  • Save and connect!

Step 4: Join the Community

For help, events, and chatting with other players, join our Discord server. We’re a friendly community eager to meet you!

Need More Help?

If you experience any issues connecting, don’t hesitate to reach out via our support email or on Discord.
