Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at The Gaming Crib. This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we collect and how it's used.

1. What We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or cookies through our website.

However, when you connect to our Minecraft servers, basic server logs may record your IP address and connection times. This is standard practice for server security, troubleshooting, and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use the Information

Information collected through server logs is used solely to:

  • Monitor and maintain server performance and security
  • Prevent cheating, abuse, or other violations of server rules
  • Ensure a fair and safe environment for all players

3. Third-Party Services

Our site may link to third-party platforms (such as our donation store or Discord server). These services have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for how they collect or use your data.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not collect identifiable personal data through this website, there is no user data to modify or delete. If you have questions related to server logs, you may contact us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@thegamingcrib.net.
